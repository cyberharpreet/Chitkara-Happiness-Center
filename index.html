CHITKARA HAPPINESS CENTRE

The Chitkara Happiness Centre stands as a beacon of well-being, providing a nurturing and supportive environment where individuals can explore and enhance their overall wellness.

Our dedicated team empowers individuals to develop self-awareness, build positive relationships, and effectively manage stress.

While traditional education prepares students for careers, the Happiness Centre prepares them for life.

Objectives of Chitkara Happiness Centre

  • Develop mindfulness, emotional intelligence, and resilience.
  • Create an inclusive and supportive environment.
  • Improve well-being using the PERMA model.
